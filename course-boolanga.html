Course “Inventory Management App Guide”

Start: September 20, 2024 • Teacher's name

Inventory is a strategic asset for organizations. Effective inventory management can minimize a company’s expenses while significantly increasing its profits.


In this course, we will look at how to use data science to manage inventory under uncertainty, how to set inventory levels based on customer service requirements, and how to calculate inventory for products with short sales cycles.
